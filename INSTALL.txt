Aether — Setup

1. Copy Aether.dll and dbghelp.dll into your Diablo II directory (next to Game.exe)
2. Copy the scripts/ folder into <game dir>/aether/scripts/
3. Launch Game.exe

That's it. dbghelp.dll is a proxy loader — Game.exe loads it automatically,
which bootstraps Aether.dll. No injector needed.

Hotkeys:
  F10  — Settings menu
  N    — Pathfind to next area
  P    — Pathfind to previous area

Wine (macOS/Linux):
  WINEDLLOVERRIDES="dbghelp=n" wine Game.exe -w

To uninstall: delete Aether.dll and dbghelp.dll from the game directory.
